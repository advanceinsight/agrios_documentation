
Odoo Community Association
============

AgriOS is built on top of the Odoo Community edition, leveraging its robust open-source ERP framework to
deliver powerful agricultural management solutions. By utilizing Odoo Community as the foundation, AgriOS
benefits from a mature, modular architecture and a large ecosystem of business applications.
